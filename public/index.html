How ReturnClaw Works

1

Tell us what to return

Speak or type what you want to return and which retailer it's from. Or just say "return the headphones" — we'll find it.

2

We check the policy

ReturnClaw instantly looks up the return window, shipping, conditions, and whether your item qualifies.

3

Connect your email

Link Gmail, Outlook, or enter an order ID. We scan for your orders and let you pick the right one.

4

Choose reason & refund

Select a return reason, pick your refund method (card, store credit, or exchange), and confirm your address.

5

We handle the rest

Get a return link, shipping label, drop-off locations or pickup scheduling, SMS tracking, and a live tracking dashboard.
